Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Data Collection

Christian Catechism does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app settings, including notification preferences, are stored locally on your device.

Notifications

The app offers an optional daily notification feature that delivers a catechism question at a time you choose. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device using local scheduling — no data is sent to any external server. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in the app settings.

Analytics

This app does not use any analytics or tracking services. Your usage of the app remains completely private.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services that could collect your data.

Children's Privacy

This app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected both here and within the app.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app store listing.
